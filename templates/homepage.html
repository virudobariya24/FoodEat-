Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about delivery, booking, or payments? Find quick answers right here.

How long does the delivery take?

Our standard delivery time is 25-35 minutes. If your order is delayed due to unexpected traffic or high order volume, you can track it live or contact customer support directly from the app.

How can I book a table at the restaurant?

Simply log in to your account, click on "Book Table" in the menu, select your preferred date, time, and number of guests, and confirm your booking. You'll receive an OTP or confirmation email instantly.

Is there a minimum order amount for delivery?

No, we do not enforce any minimum order values. You can order as little or as much as you like! However, free delivery applies to orders above ₹200.

What payment options are available?

We support a wide range of secure payment options including Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Netbanking, and Cash on Delivery (COD).
