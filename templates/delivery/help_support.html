Frequently Asked Questions

How do weekly payouts work?

Weekly rider earnings and tips are automatically calculated and processed every Sunday night. Payouts are directly transferred to the bank account registered in your Bank Details tab on Monday morning.

How do I accept delivery tasks?

Make sure you toggle your **Duty Status** inside the sidebar driver card to **ONLINE**. Once online, active orders prepared by restaurants in your local area will appear in your **Available Requests** panel, where you can click **Accept Task**.

What is the customer OTP verification?

For security reasons, every delivery must be verified. When you arrive at the customer's address, request the customer's delivery OTP code, input it in the active task panel, and verify to mark the order as delivered.

What if the customer is unreachable?

If the customer is not answering calls or is unreachable at the address, try calling their phone via the green **Call Customer** shortcut in your dashboard. If there is no response after 10 minutes, contact the support hotline immediately.
